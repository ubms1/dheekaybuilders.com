Who We Are

Building the Philippines' Future, One Structure at a Time

Established in 2026, DHEEKAY BUILDERS OPC is a premier construction company headquartered in Lemu Norte, Enrile, Cagayan, Philippines. We specialize in telecommunications tower construction and various engineering structures, delivering world-class quality to every project we undertake.

As a PCAB-licensed contractor with ongoing ISO certification, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and professionalism in the construction industry. Our team of experienced engineers, architects, and skilled workers brings expertise and dedication to every project.

From residential homes to commercial buildings, from institutional facilities to complex engineering structures, we have the capability and experience to handle diverse construction requirements. Our flagship service—Telco Tower Construction—positions us at the forefront of the Philippines' digital infrastructure development.
