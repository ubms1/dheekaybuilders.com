Who We Are

DHEEKAY BUILDERS OPC

DHEEKAY BUILDERS OPC is a premier construction company based in Lemu Norte, Enrile, Cagayan, Philippines. Established in 2026, we have rapidly built a reputation for excellence in telecommunications tower construction and general building works.

As a PCAB Licensed contractor with ISO certification in progress, we are committed to delivering world-class construction services that meet the highest quality and safety standards. Our specialization in telco tower construction positions us as a trusted partner for major telecommunications operators across the Philippines.

Our team of skilled engineers, project managers, and construction professionals brings expertise, dedication, and innovation to every project we undertake.
