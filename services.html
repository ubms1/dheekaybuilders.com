Our Construction Services

DHEEKAY BUILDERS OPC provides comprehensive construction solutions with a flagship specialization in telco tower construction and telecommunications infrastructure.

Telco Tower Construction

Our flagship service — complete telecommunications tower construction from site preparation to commissioning. We build self-supporting towers, guyed towers, monopoles, and rooftop structures for major telecom operators across the Philippines.

  • Self-Supporting Towers (SST)
  • Guyed Mast Towers
  • Monopole Installations
  • Rooftop Structures
  • Antenna Mounting & Cabling
  • Tower Foundation Works
  • Site Grounding Systems
  • Lightning Protection

Commercial Construction

Full-service commercial building construction including offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and mixed-use developments. We deliver modern, functional structures that meet the needs of Philippine businesses.

  • Retail Developments
  • Warehouse Construction

  • Interior Fit-Out
  • Building Renovation

Residential Construction
