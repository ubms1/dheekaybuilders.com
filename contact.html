FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our construction services and processes.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Cagayan Valley region and accept projects across Luzon, Philippines. Our telco tower services are available nationwide.

Are you a licensed contractor?

Yes, DHEEKAY BUILDERS OPC is fully licensed by the PCAB (Philippine Contractors Accreditation Board), and our ISO certification is currently in progress.

How do I get a project quotation?

Simply fill out our contact form, call us at 0905 864 2613, or email us. We provide free initial consultations and competitive quotes within 48 hours.

What types of telco towers do you build?

We construct self-supporting towers (SST), guyed towers, monopoles, rooftop structures, and small cell poles. Each design is engineered to specifications.

Do you handle permits and documentation?

Yes, we assist with building permits, environmental compliance certificates, barangay clearances, and all necessary construction documentation.

What safety standards do you follow?

We comply with DOLE OSH standards, implement zero-accident policies, conduct regular safety training, and maintain comprehensive safety protocols on all sites.
